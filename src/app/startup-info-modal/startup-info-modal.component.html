About this application

PSC Prevent is a machine learning based risk management plattform that supports our quality assurance team to be better prepared for upcoming PSCs. I originally built this tool for my current company.

What are Port State Controls?

Port State Controls are based on an international agreement, which allows port authorities to inspect incoming ships at any time, identify deficiencies and, in worst case, even reserve the right to detain it if the deficiencies are considered critical.
